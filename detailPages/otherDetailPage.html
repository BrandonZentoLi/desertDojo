Egg Tarts

Definitely my favourite dessert. A little similar to creme brulee, egg or custard tart is found in Cantonese cusine and originated inthe Guandong province. Egg tarts conist of a outer pastry crust filled with sweet egg custard.

Dango

A Japanese very similar to the mochi mentioned earlier. This dessert is made of rice flour mixed with glutinous rice flour. Often served on skewers, with 3 or 5 per skewer.
